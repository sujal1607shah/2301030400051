6-Shooter-Arduino-Drink-Mixing-Station

Step 1: Gather Materials

Collect the necessary components, including the Arduino board, motors, sensors, wires, and 6 drink dispensers.

Step 2: Setup the Circuit

Wire the motors and sensors to the Arduino board, ensuring each component is connected correctly for smooth operation.

Step 3: Program the Arduino

Write and upload the Arduino code to control the drink dispensers based on user input or sensor data.

Step 4: Assemble the Dispensers

Attach the motors to the drink dispensers and secure the system in place for stable operation during mixing.

Step 5: Test the System

Run a series of tests to ensure the dispensers work as expected, with accurate control over the mixing process.

Step 6: Fine-tune and Optimize

Make any necessary adjustments to the Arduino code, motor speeds, or dispenser setups to ensure optimal performance.
